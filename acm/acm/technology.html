ACM's Technology

ACM is all about using the best of search engine techniques. Search is already integral to our lives, so ACM seeks to apply these techniques in a way that can help us analyse how to pursue that dream job better.

Vector Space Model

More than just comparing the similarity of documents, the vector space model is integral in comparing the similarities of CVs and job descriptions. This enables ACM to compare and evaluate the capabilities of candidates and profile them accordingly
